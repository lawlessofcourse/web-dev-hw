About:

Hi! My name is Jack Lawless and I chose to do a How To Make A Splitboard because I have wanted to do this myself and get into the backcountry. I love snowboarding and want to get into backcountry snowboarding, but it can be a bit pricey. So, I did some research and found out that local shops will split boards for you and wondered if there was a how to. There was! I found the instructions on making a splitboard from Transworld snowboarding and based the page around it. I also decided to include links to local shops in Missoula for people who don't have the tools and resources to complete the Instructable. And I also included links to websites that provide further information on backcountry skiing and the proper equipment to do so. Backcountry skiing can be expensive! Try to find deals and used gear! I hope this page helped further your voyage into the mountains!
